Unique Health ID: A 16-Digit Code to Unlock Your Health Records

At the heart of the health card is a unique 16-digit Health ID number, known as the ABHA number. This identifier ensures that your health records are secure, up-to-date, and accessible across multiple healthcare providers.

Health Address: Your Personalized Username

When you register for your health card, you'll create a self-declared username, known as your health address. This is similar to an email address and serves as your login credentials for the Health Information Exchange and consent manager.
